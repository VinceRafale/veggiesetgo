Travel beyond the vegetarian restaurant

VeggieSetGo is where veggie lovers and omnivores can find restaurants to dine out together – anywhere.

Introducing the Artichoke Rating

The artichoke rating shows how vegetarian-friendly a restaurant is on a scale of 1 (1-2 veggie dishes) to 5 (plenty of veggie options to choose from). Restaurants that are completely vegetarian aren't given an artichoke rating – you'll see them marked with a "100% Vegetarian" badge.
